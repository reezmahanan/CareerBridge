Press Release  ·  March 15, 2026

CareerBridge Raises $50M Series C to Expand Global Footprint

CareerBridge Communications March 15, 2026 4 min read

CareerBridge, the leading talent-matching platform connecting professionals with top employers worldwide, today announced it has raised $50 million in Series C funding led by Sequoia Capital, with participation from Andreessen Horowitz and existing investors.

Accelerating Global Growth

The new funding will be used to accelerate product development, expand the platform's AI capabilities, and launch operations in 10 new markets across Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. The company currently serves over 500,000 job seekers and 10,000 companies in 15 countries.

"This investment is a powerful validation of our mission to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity," said James Wilson, Co-Founder and CEO of CareerBridge. "We're just getting started. With this funding, we'll bring our platform to millions more professionals and thousands more companies around the world."

Record Growth Metrics

CareerBridge has seen remarkable traction since its Series B round in 2024:

  • 3x revenue growth year-over-year
  • 500,000+ registered job seekers (up from 150,000)
  • 10,000+ employer partners (up from 3,200)
  • 50,000+ successful placements to date
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS) of 72 — industry-leading

Investor Perspective

"CareerBridge has built something special — a platform that genuinely improves outcomes for both job seekers and employers," said a Partner at Sequoia Capital. "Their AI-powered matching technology creates real value, and we're excited to support their next phase of growth."

About CareerBridge

CareerBridge is a talent marketplace that uses AI and data to match professionals with their ideal roles. Founded in 2022 and headquartered in San Francisco, the company's platform helps individuals navigate their careers and helps organizations build world-class teams.

For media inquiries, contact: press@careerbridge.com
Product News  ·  February 28, 2026

CareerBridge Launches AI-Powered Candidate Matching

Sarah Chen, CTO February 28, 2026 5 min read

CareerBridge today unveiled its next-generation AI Matching Engine — a proprietary machine learning system that reduces average time-to-hire by 68% and delivers candidates with 3x higher job fit scores compared to traditional keyword-search methods.

How It Works

The AI Matching Engine analyzes over 200 data signals — including skills, experience trajectory, work style preferences, cultural values, and career goals — to surface the right candidates for every role.

Unlike legacy ATS systems that rely purely on keyword matching, our engine understands context. It learns which candidate attributes predict long-term success at each company, and improves continuously with every hire made on the platform.

Key Results from Beta

  • 68% reduction in time-to-hire for beta employers
  • 3x higher candidate quality scores vs. keyword search
  • 89% of beta candidates rated their job fit "excellent" at 6-month mark
  • 45% decrease in early attrition for AI-matched hires

Available Now

The AI Matching Engine is available immediately to all Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers. Starter plan users can access a limited version with up to 5 AI-matched candidates per job post.

"We've essentially turned 10 years of placement data into an unfair advantage for both job seekers and employers," said Sarah Chen, Co-Founder and CTO. "The right match changes lives — and now we can make that happen faster and more accurately than ever."

CareerBridge Named in Forbes' Best Startup Employers 2026

Michael Adebayo, COO January 10, 2026 3 min read

CareerBridge has been recognized in Forbes' prestigious Best Startup Employers 2026 list, ranking #7 among the top 500 startups for employee satisfaction, company culture, and growth opportunities.

Recognition Built on Real Feedback

The Forbes list is compiled from an independent survey of over 300,000 employees at U.S. startups, assessing employer image, people & culture, diversity, and employee development. CareerBridge achieved an overall satisfaction score of 94/100.

Why It Matters
